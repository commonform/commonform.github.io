Common Form

composable, verifiable, shareable legal contracts

Common Form lets attorneys, companies, and computer systems share forms, provisions, definitions, and other contract components free of client confidential information. It is also a set of tools that manage contract structure, leaving you free to focus on substance.

Common Form is under active development. A preview of the project's web-based interface is online at commonform.org. If you are adept at command-line computing, you can use the command-line interface. If you would like to contribute code, considering tackling an open issue.
